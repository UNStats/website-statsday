18 November is African Statistics Day

10 Sep 2015



On 18 November every year, African countries celebrate African Statistics Day (ASD) to raise public awareness about the importance of statistics in supporting decision making in all dimensions of sustainable development. This celebration provides an opportunity for each country to take stock of its achievements and challenges in monitoring progress towards national, regional and global development goals.

Open Russian Statistical Congress

19 Aug 2015



In October 2015, the Russian Association of Statisticians (RASt) will hold the Open Russian Statistical Congress, the first of its kind in modern Russia. It will kick off on October 20, 2015 which coincides with the World Statistics Day 2015 and will last for two days in Novosibirsk city. The co-organisers include the Russian statistical office (ROSSTAT) and the Novosibirsk State University of Economics and Management. The congress is open for professionals and everyone who is interested in statistics.

General Assembly adopts World Statistics Day Resolution

03 Jun 2015

On 3 June 2015, the United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution officially designating 20 October 2015 as the second World Statistics Day, under the general theme “Better data. Better lives”. In addition, the General Assembly decided to celebrate World Statistics Day on 20 October every five years.
