World Statistics Day in China

World Statistics Day was celebrated in Xi'an/China with an impressive ceremony in the presence of Mr. Wu Hongbo, Under-Secretary-General for Economic and Social Affairs, UN Department of Economic and Social Affairs.

This event provided a timely and excellent opportunity for the global statistical community to gather and exchange experiences and discuss critical issues on how to develop an effective monitoring framework to support the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.
