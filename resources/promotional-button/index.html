Promotional button design available for download

10 Jun 2015



During the official launch of the World Statistics Day 2015 campaign at the 46th session of the United Nations Statistical Commission, the UN Statistics Division distributed among delegates promotional buttons depicting the official logo with a smiling face. Feel free to use the graphic files available from the links below to print your own buttons.

  • Vector graphics file format
  • Portable document format

Please keep us posted at statistics@un.org about any promotional material that you created using the logos. We are happy to help you promote your World Statistics Day 2015 event.
