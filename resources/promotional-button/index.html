Promotional button design available for download

10 Jun 2015



During the official launch of the World Statistics Day 2015 campaign at the 46th session of the United Nations Statistical Commission, the UN Statistics Division distributed among delegates promotional buttons depicting the official logo with a smiling face. Feel free to use the graphic files available from the links below to print your own buttons.

  • Vector graphics file format
  • Portable document format

Please keep us posted at statistics@un.org about any promotional material that you created using the logos. We are happy to help you promote your World Statistics Day 2015 event.

WSD logo now available in Estonian, Hungarian, Lithuanian and Welsh

14 Sep 2015




With the release of these four new versions, the logo of World Statistics Day 2015 is now available in 19 different languages. Feel free to use all of them, and become part of a truly global celebration on 20 October 2015. To get the logo in your language, please contact UNSD at statistics@un.org and provide the translation of all language in the logo into your language. UNSD in cooperation with the Graphic Design Unit of the UN will provide the logo in various file formats.

World Statistics Day 2015 Logos

26 May 2015

The official logos of World Statistics Day 2015 are available for download in multiple languages, including the six official languages of the United Nations: Arabic, Chinese, English, French, Russian, and Spanish. In addition, the logo has already been translated into many more languages!
