Campaign to collect interesting facts about NSOs being carried out by Rosstat as part of their World Statistics Day celebrations!


24 September 2020

On occasion of the third World Statistics Day the Federal State Statistics Service of the Russian Federation (Rosstat) has launched a campaign on collecting interesting facts about NSOs to present them further on in the shape of diagrams and charts visualizing the differences. On the other hand, Rosstat has also disseminated the questionnaire among heads of NSOs and International organizations. The collected replies in the form of an interview along with interesting facts will be published on a thematic page of the Rosstat website. The idea is to attract attention of the audience to the WSD in a soft, gentle and entertaining form. We invite everyone to contribute to the initiative and tell about their career in statistics or about the NSO.

Should you have any questions, please, feel free to reach Rosstat via international@gks.ru.
