Hari Statistika Dunia will be celebrated in Indonesia

22 Sep 2015



Hari Statistika Dunia or World Statistics Day can be now celebrated in Indonesia with our logo newly available in Bahasa Indonesia, the official language of Indonesia. Submissions in any language are welcome. Please send them to statistics@un.org

The Swiss Statistical Society will celebrate World Statistics Day with a seminar on "Better data. Better lives."

22 Sep 2015

To celebrate the second World Statistics Day and to further strengthen its visibility, the Swiss Statistical Society is organizing an event on 20 October 2015.

article

European Central Bank will celebrate World Statistics Day with a conference entitled "European Statistics by the European System of Central Banks"

22 Sep 2015

To celebrate this year’s World Statistics Day, the European Central Bank is organizing and hosting a conference on the “European Statistics by the European System of Central Banks”. The main purpose of the conference is to present the European economic and financial statistics by the European System of Central Banks (ESCB), achievements and further developments.
