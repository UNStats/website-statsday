World Statistics Day 2015 Logos

The official logos of World Statistics Day 2015 are available for download in multiple languages, including the six official languages of the United Nations.

You are welcome to translate the logo into your own language. Send your translation to statistics@un.org and we will add the new language version to this page. Governments, intergovernmental organizations, not-for-profit organizations, and private sector entities may use the logos in accordance with the liability waiver and the graphic standards.

Please keep us posted at statistics@un.org about any promotional material that you created using the logos. We are happy to help you promote your World Statistics Day 2015 event.
