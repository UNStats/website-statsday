A new Statistics Day poster in Japanese is out

18 Aug 2015



20 October 2015 is the second World Statistics day. But did you know that 18 October this year is also the Japanese Statistics Day?

To promote both celebrations, the government of Japan has released a poster featuring the image of the World Statistics Day 2015 logo and the Japanese translation of the slogan “Better data. Better lives.”

Statistics Bureau of Japan kicks off its World Statistics Day 2015 campaign with commemorative mug

05 Jun 2015



The Statistics Bureau of Japan has created a mug showcasing the logo of World Statistics Day 2015, side by side with its own organization logo and the mascots of Japan’s Population Census to be conducted in October 2015. The mugs are a great way of inviting conference participants, contributors to census and guests to the Statistics Bureau of Japan to join the celebration of the World Statistics Day 2015.
